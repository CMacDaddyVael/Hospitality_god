# Hospitality Paid Advertising Knowledge Base (2025-2026)

## Google Ads

### Performance Max for Hotels
- Google's "Power Pack": Demand Gen (awareness) + AI Max for Search (intent) + Performance Max (full-funnel)
- Hotels running all three see strongest results
- PMax supports Travel Goals with Hotel Center feed integration (live rates + availability in ads)

### Demand Gen with Travel Feeds
- As of Jan 2026, Demand Gen supports Hotel Center feed integration
- Dynamic video ads with pricing, ratings, availability
- Multi-format creative (video + images) drives **20% more conversions** at equivalent CPA vs video-only

### Brand Bidding: Non-Negotiable
- 250,000+ Google ads across North America target hotel brand names
- Bidding on own brand terms yields **30% increase in total clicks**
- Prevents high-commission leakage to OTAs

### Key Benchmarks
- Median hotel ROAS on Google Ads: **15.19x** (April 2025, fluctuates)
- Luxury hotels: 6:1 to 10:1 ROAS; mid-tier: 4:1 to 6:1
- Google Ads CPC risen **62.5%** for hotel searches
- Search campaigns: lowest CPA (8%), followed by Hotel Ads (10%) and Display (10%)

### Non-Brand Paid Search
- 13% increase in direct bookings, 10% reduction in OTA bookings
- Critical window: 4-10 weeks before travel (general research phase)

## Meta (Facebook/Instagram)

### What Converts
- Reels ads are highest-performing format in 2026
- 15-30 second video outperforms static by 40%
- BUT static images still drive **60-70% of actual conversions** — don't abandon them
- UGC and authentic lifestyle imagery converts **4.5% better** than stock
- Reels engagement growing 22% YoY

### Funnel Architecture
- Split: **40% awareness / 60% conversion+retargeting**
- Retargeted visitors are **70% more likely to convert**
- Dynamic retargeting with live rates from booking engine is critical for bottom-funnel

### Audience Strategy (Andromeda Era)
- Creative IS the targeting signal now — algorithm uses creative to find audience
- More creative variants = broader, more efficient reach
- Upload loyalty lists, past guest emails, website visitors for Custom Audiences
- Build Lookalikes from best-converting segments
- First-party data essential since third-party cookie targeting degraded

## TikTok

### TikTok Travel Ads (Launched October 2025)
- Dedicated ad product using AI to identify travel-intent users
- Hotels upload content library, TikTok AI matches visuals/offers to right audience
- **Early mover window before CPMs rise**

### Performance Data
- **32% of consumers** have booked accommodation discovered on TikTok
- Smaller hotel profiles see engagement rates above 100%, avg 89K views/post
- 67% of TikTok users search intent-driven queries ("Where to stay in [destination]")
- AI-powered itinerary content sees **40% higher CTR**

### Tactical Approach
- Post 3-5x per week
- Long-term creator partnerships (trust > follower count)
- Authentic room tours, behind-the-scenes, destination storytelling over polished brand videos

## Programmatic & CTV

### Connected TV
- $37 billion projected CTV ad spend 2026
- 90% of US households reachable via CTV
- Precise targeting by demographic, household segment, behavioral audience
- Especially powerful for luxury/resort awareness campaigns

### Programmatic Display
- US programmatic display exceeds $203 billion in 2026 (+12.5% YoY)
- Three pillars in cookieless world: first-party data, alternative IDs, contextual intelligence
- Curated deals (41% cite as path to higher ROI) beat open exchange for quality travel placements

## Attribution

### Core Challenges
- Cross-site identity loss breaks multi-touch attribution
- Multi-device journeys create gaps
- Lost conversions misattributed to "Direct"
- 30-90+ day booking windows make last-click misleading

### Solutions That Work
- **Server-side tracking** recovers **15-30% of lost conversion signals** (bypasses ad blockers)
- AI attribution models (Markov Chains, Shapley Values) assign value without personal identifiers
- Multi-method stack: consent management + server-side tagging + data clean rooms
- Move from channel-level ROAS to incremental contribution measurement

## Budget Allocation by Property Type

### Overall Benchmarks
- Hotels: 8-15% of annual revenue to marketing total
- New/rebranded properties: closer to 15%
- 25-35% of marketing budget to digital paid (search, metasearch, social)
- 20-25% to website optimization, SEO, content

### Luxury Resorts
- Heavier upper-funnel: CTV, programmatic, Meta awareness
- Can tolerate higher CPA given ADR
- Target: **60% brand/awareness, 40% performance**

### Boutique / Independent Hotels
- Brand bidding is existential
- Prioritize Google Hotel Ads and metasearch (75% of travelers use metasearch)
- Lean into Meta retargeting with authentic imagery
- Target: **30% brand/awareness, 70% performance**

### Vacation Rentals
- Stricter CPA control (lower margins)
- Google Search and metasearch are primary channels
- Social for repeat bookings and referrals
- Target: **20% brand/awareness, 80% performance**

### Key Trend
Move away from fixed quarterly allocations toward **real-time, performance-based budget rebalancing**

## Competing with OTAs

### The Stakes
- OTAs: **63% of all online hotel reservations** (Phocuswright)
- OTA commissions: 15-25% per booking (18-23% net revenue)
- **26% of travelers now start hotel search on Booking.com**, overtaking Google
- Metasearch costs have more than doubled

### Tactical Playbook
1. **Brand defense mandatory** — 30% click uplift covers CPC vs losing to 20% OTA commission
2. **Rate parity + added value** — can't undercut OTA rates, but offer direct perks
3. **Metasearch is primary battleground** — 75% of travelers use it; Google eliminated commission bidding, shift to CPC/CPA
4. **Capture research phase** — non-brand keywords 4-10 weeks before travel
5. **First-party data moat** — past guest retargeting costs fraction of OTA acquisition
6. **Website conversion optimization** — best-in-class: 3-4% conversion; most below 2%; every 1% improvement compounds across all channels

- Metasearch (massively under-utilized relative to 75% traveler usage)
- Creative diversity in Meta (functions as targeting now)
- Campaign total budgets (new in Google Ads 2026 — ideal for seasonal promotions)
