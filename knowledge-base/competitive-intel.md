# Competitive Intelligence (March 2026)

## Key Finding
No AI CMO exists for anyone — hotels or STR owners. This is a new category.

## Direct Competitors (AI-Powered)

| Platform | Focus | Pricing | Gap |
|----------|-------|---------|-----|
| Revinate | Hotel CRM + email marketing + AI email copy | From $150/mo | Email-only, no ads/SEO/social |
| Cendyn | Enterprise hotel CRM + CDP + AI Connect for LLM distribution | Enterprise custom | Enterprise-only, no execution |
| Cloudbeds | PMS + basic marketing (AI review responses, retargeting) | From ~$24/mo | Marketing is a bolt-on, very basic |
| Profitroom | Booking engine + marketing automation | Custom | European focus, conversion-only |
| happyhotel | AI agents for hotel revenue management (€6.5M Series A Feb 2026) | Unknown | Revenue management only, not marketing |

## STR-Specific Tools (None Do Marketing)

| Platform | Focus | Gap |
|----------|-------|-----|
| Hostaway | PMS + channel manager | Operations, not marketing |
| Guesty | PMS + channel manager | Operations, not marketing |
| Hospitable | Guest messaging automation | Messaging only, no marketing |
| PriceLabs / Wheelhouse / Beyond | Dynamic pricing | Pricing only |
| AirDNA | Market data + analytics | Data only, no execution |

**Nobody is doing marketing execution for STR owners. The market is wide open.**

## Indirect Competitors

### Hotel Marketing Agencies (Human)
- Thrive, Gourmet Marketing, Mediaboom, Bay Leaf Digital
- Pricing: $3K-15K/mo (inaccessible to STR owners)
- Full-service but expensive, slow, human-dependent

### Legacy Software
- SiteMinder: $85/mo (channel management)
- HubSpot/Salesforce: $50-1,200+/mo (general CRM, not hospitality-specific)

## What STR Owners Pay Today
- Marketing agency: $0 (can't afford one)
- Airbnb/Vrbo fees: 3-15% per booking
- PMS software: $20-100/mo
- Dynamic pricing: $10-30/mo per listing
- Total marketing spend: effectively $0

## Market Gaps for VAEL Host
1. **No AI CMO exists** — point solutions only (email OR CRM OR revenue)
2. **STR owners have zero marketing support** — no tool or agency serves them
3. **Content generation is manual everywhere** — even "AI" platforms only do email copy
4. **GEO is wide open** — Cendyn AI Connect is enterprise-only; nobody optimizes STR for AI search
5. **Fragmented stack** — owners need 4+ tools; we can be one
6. **2026 is the inflection** — AI moving from experimental to embedded in hospitality
