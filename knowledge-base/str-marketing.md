# STR Marketing Knowledge Base (March 2026)

## Airbnb Listing Optimization

### Title Optimization
- Front-load the most compelling feature: "Oceanfront Condo with Pool" not "Beautiful 2BR"
- Include location landmarks and unique selling points
- Airbnb search algorithm weighs title keywords heavily

### Description Best Practices
- First 2 sentences are critical (preview text before "Show more")
- Structure: hook → key amenities → neighborhood → logistics
- Use specific details ("5-minute walk to beach" not "close to beach")
- Include seasonal context ("heated pool for year-round swimming")

### Tags & Amenities
- Complete every amenity checkbox — algorithm uses these for filtered searches
- Missing amenities = missing from search results when guests filter
- Highlight: WiFi speed, workspace, EV charging, pet-friendly, self check-in

## Photography That Converts

### Impact Data
- Professional photos increase earnings by **40%** and bookings by **24%** (Airbnb data)
- Properties with **30+ high-quality photos** book **2x as often** as those with fewer
- **80% of guests** decide whether to explore a listing based on the first 5 photos

### Photo Strategy
- Aim for 20-30 photos minimum
- Resolution: at least 1024×683 pixels
- Always landscape orientation (fits Airbnb platform better)
- First 5 photos are everything: hero exterior → living room → kitchen → primary bedroom → best amenity (pool/view/deck)

### Shooting Tips
- Natural light, all curtains open
- Turn on all lamps for warmth even during daytime
- Wide-angle lens for spaciousness
- Stage with lifestyle touches: book on nightstand, coffee on table, fresh flowers
- Remove all clutter and personal items
- Show the experience, not just the space

## Review Management

### Getting 5-Star Reviews
- Ask immediately after checkout while experience is fresh
- Mid-stay check-in message ("How's everything going?") catches problems before they become bad reviews — turns potential 4-star into 5-star
- Respond to ALL issues within minutes, offer to make it right
- Spotless cleanliness is the #1 factor in reviews

### Review Response Strategy
- Respond to every review (positive and negative)
- Positive: thank specifically, mention something personal about their stay
- Negative: acknowledge, explain what you've fixed, invite them back
- Reviews with responses signal active management to future guests

### Automated Review Requests
- Send automated post-stay message within 2 hours of checkout
- Include direct link to leave review
- Keep request short and personal
- Mention a specific moment from their stay if possible

## Guest Communication Sequences

### The 5-Message Framework
1. **Booking confirmation** (immediate): Thank them, confirm dates, set expectations
2. **Pre-arrival** (3-7 days before): Check-in instructions, local recommendations, build excitement
3. **Check-in day** (morning of): Door codes, WiFi password, emergency contact, "we're here if you need anything"
4. **Mid-stay check-in** (day 2-3): "How's everything? Need anything?" — catches problems early
5. **Post-checkout** (within 2 hours): Thank them, ask for review, mention direct booking discount for next time

### Automation Tools STR Owners Currently Use
- Hospitable (formerly Smartbnb): $25-40/mo
- Host Tools: $5-10/mo per listing
- Guesty: $30+/mo
- Built-in Airbnb scheduled messages (basic, free)

## Direct Booking Strategy

### Why It Matters
- Airbnb takes 3% host fee + 14% guest fee (effectively ~17% total)
- Vrbo takes 5% host fee or 8% total fee
- Direct bookings keep 100% minus payment processing (~3%)
- Even converting 20% of repeat guests to direct saves thousands/year

### How to Build Direct Bookings
- Capture guest emails (StayFi WiFi marketing: from $15/mo, captures all guest emails via branded WiFi portal)
- Send post-stay emails with direct booking discount (10-15% off platform price)
- Build simple direct booking website (Lodgify, Hostfully, or custom)
- Include business card in welcome packet with direct booking URL
- Google Business Profile for your property (free, shows in local search)
