# Hospitality God — Vision & Goals

## Mission
Build an autonomous AI-powered CMO that actually EXECUTES hospitality marketing — not advises. Hotels sign up, connect their accounts, and the agent runs their marketing: content, ads, SEO, social, email, reviews, everything.

## Target
3,000 hotel clients by end of 2027 (~21 months from March 2026)

## Segments
- Hotels (branded & independent)
- Resorts
- Boutique properties
- Vacation rentals

## What "Done" Looks Like
A hotel signs up, connects their accounts (Meta, Google Ads, GBP, email platform, TikTok, analytics), and the agent autonomously:
- Creates and posts social content (with VAEL-generated visuals)
- Writes and sends email sequences
- Manages Google Ads and Meta campaigns
- Optimizes GBP listings and responds to reviews
- Generates SEO/GEO content (blog posts, landing pages, schema markup)
- Pulls performance data and adjusts strategy
- Reports results back to the client

## Principles
- Execution over advice — the agent DOES the work, not recommends it
- Focus on independent/boutique properties first (underserved, can't afford a real CMO)
- Knowledge base stays current autonomously (research loop)
- Every tactic must be grounded in data and benchmarks
- Do NOT contact leads yet — build the product first

## Timeline
- **April 2026:** Start selling. MVP must be live — onboarding, at least 2-3 execution channels working, landing page up.
- **Q2 2026:** First 50 paying clients
- **Q4 2026:** 300 clients
- **2027:** Scale to 3,000
